The Legend of Zane's Tower

Welcome thee to the Tower of Zane
The building of which drove Zane insane

Trapped are thee, but have no fear
Three ways there are to get thee clear

Many rooms in this tower reside
Many tall, many wide

If each room you visit in turn
The way out will open upon your return

If every beastie and ghoul you slay
Then may you see the light of day

Finally, three items herein are key
Place in this box, and you'll be free!
